SCHEHEREZADE
Scheherazade is a legendary Persian queen and the storyteller of One Thousand and One Nights.
Shehrazad was a girl of quick mind who had learned a great many famous fairy tales. She was also strong, kind and good hearted.
The story goes that every day Shahryar (king) would marry a new virgin, and after doing so would despatch the previous day's wife to be beheaded. 
He had killed 1000 such women by the time he was introduced to Scheherazade, the daughter of the vizier.
